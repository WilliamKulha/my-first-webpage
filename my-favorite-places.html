Here Are My Favorite Places

  1. Sapporo The largest city in Hokkaido, famous for beer and Miso Ramen.
    If you like Ramen check out Red Star Ramen.
  2. Sarasota My hometown, famous for its whitesand beaches and arts culture.
    If you like home-cooking check you the The Metro Diner.
  3. New York City What more can be said that hasn't already been said? If you can make it there you can make it anywhere.
    For a great cup of coffee and some light food, check out Cafe Angelique .
  4. Seattle My sister lives there, as did I years ago. A great city that doesn't even feel very much like a city.
    Check out local landmark shop Uwajimaya in the international district.

Thanks for taking the time to read my list!
